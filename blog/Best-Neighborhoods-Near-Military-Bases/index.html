Best Neighborhoods Near Military Bases in San Antonio: 2025 Relocation Guide

June 7, 2025 8 min read San Antonio, TX

San Antonio is more than a military city—it's a place where service members and their families build lives, not just careers. With Joint Base San Antonio comprising Lackland AFB, Fort Sam Houston, and Randolph AFB, housing needs are as diverse as the missions being carried out.

In 2025, the real estate market near these bases offers a range of options, whether you're a first-time VA buyer, a relocating officer, or retiring into civilian life. This guide highlights the best neighborhoods near San Antonio military bases, complete with commute times, VA loan insights, and what makes each area ideal for buyers who value community, security, and investment potential.

"San Antonio isn't just where you're stationed—it's where you plant roots. Choose your neighborhood wisely and you'll find a community that supports your military lifestyle while building equity for your future."

Lackland AFB Area: West San Antonio LACKLAND

Base Commute

10–20 minutes to Lackland AFB gates

Top Neighborhoods:

  • Alamo Ranch - Most in-demand for military families
  • Westover Hills - Established community with mature trees
  • Medina Valley (Castroville) - Rural charm with excellent schools

Why It Works for Military Families:

These communities offer highly rated NISD schools in Alamo Ranch and Medina Valley ISD. Many homes are new construction, qualifying easily for VA appraisals with minimal repairs or contingencies.

VA Buyer Advantage: Alamo Ranch builders frequently offer VA loan incentives and rate buy-downs for active duty service members. The area's new construction means fewer appraisal concerns.

Bonus: Alamo Ranch is one of the most in-demand areas for military families—strong resale value and amenities like shopping, parks, and access to Loop 1604 make it ideal for frequent PCS moves.

Fort Sam Houston Area: Central San Antonio FORT SAM

Base Commute

5–15 minutes to Fort Sam Houston gates

Top Neighborhoods:

  • Terrell Hills - Officer preferred with historic homes
  • Alamo Heights - Top-tier schools, closer to post
  • Windcrest - Affordable and quiet, short drive

Why It Works for Military Families:

Urban, walkable options with historic charm near the medical center. Excellent private school options complement the strong public schools in Alamo Heights ISD.

VA Loan Tip: Some historic homes in this area may need updates; make sure your agent is VA-savvy to navigate inspection requirements. Windcrest offers the most VA-friendly inventory.

Bonus: Alamo Heights offers prestige and access to excellent schools, while Windcrest is a hidden gem for budget-conscious military families wanting to be close to base.

Randolph AFB Area: Northeast San Antonio RANDOLPH

Base Commute

10–20 minutes via I-35 or Loop 1604

Top Neighborhoods:

  • Cibolo - Fast-growing with new developments
  • Schertz - Strong military community
  • Selma - Convenient to both Randolph and Fort Sam

Why It Works for Military Families:

These communities have a strong military presence with family-friendly parks and schools. Schertz-Cibolo-Universal City ISD (SCUC ISD) is one of the best school districts for military families in Texas.

VA Loan Tip: Many builders in these areas offer VA loan incentives and rate buy-downs for active duty service members. The military community means most sellers understand VA loan timelines.

Bonus: The Randolph area offers more square footage for your money compared to central San Antonio, with easy base access via multiple routes.

💡 VA Loan Insights for San Antonio Military Buyers

2025 VA Loan Limit: No cap with full entitlement Down Payment: 0% down VA financing available Appraisal Tip: New builds = smoother VA approval

Navigating the VA loan process in San Antonio's competitive 2025 market requires specialized knowledge:

  • Timing is everything: Many military families compete for the same homes near bases. Having a VA-savvy agent who can move quickly is essential.
  • Appraisal awareness: The VA's Minimum Property Requirements (MPRs) can affect older homes. Newer developments near Lackland and Randolph typically have fewer issues.
  • Funding fee changes: 2025 brings updated VA funding fee percentages—your Orion agent can calculate how this affects your purchase.

🚀 Base-Specific Home Buying Strategies

Lackland AFB Families

Prioritize neighborhoods with flexible lease-back options if you're buying before your PCS is complete. Many Alamo Ranch sellers will accommodate military timelines.

Fort Sam Houston Medical Corps

Consider proximity to SAMMC when choosing between Terrell Hills and Alamo Heights. The difference of 5 minutes in commute can mean significant price differences.

Randolph AFB Aviators

Schertz offers the best balance of school quality and commute time, with many homes featuring noise-reducing features for the flight path areas.
